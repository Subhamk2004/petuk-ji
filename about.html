Who Are We

We are a multinational startup providing a one-stop solution for food and grocery delivery. Supported by the UP Government, Google, and MeitY, we stand out as entrepreneurs in the food industry. In 2021, we proudly earned recognition as one of India's top 100 startups, showcasing our commitment to convenience and excellence in the delivery marketplace.

What We Do


We connect local sellers and buyers to deliver fresh products directly to your door. Whether you’re looking to order food from a restaurant, fresh produce, or fruits, we’ve got you covered. Our mission is to make shopping easier and more convenient by connecting you directly with local sellers.

Our Focus

At Petuk Ji, we transcend the traditional definition of a company, we are a dynamic marketplace, energizing networked digital commerce. Our focus is on empowering driven entrepreneurs to seamlessly establish their ventures within the food and grocery delivery sector, fostering collaborations with local vendors. We present versatile avenues for participation, enabling aspiring business owners to either embrace a comprehensive model or specialize within specific verticals.
